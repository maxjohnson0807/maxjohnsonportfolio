Additional Games Developed

More games I developed in Unreal Engine 5 and Unity, showcasing my creative approaches to building games in different genres.

Survivor: Space Wreckage

Survivor: Space Wreckage is a short platformer/shooter with a focus on visuals. This was my first game made in Unreal Engine 5. The goal is to platform your way to the end of the level dodging asteroids and remote turrets and not falling off.

Interloper

Interloper is my first major game built in Unity. It was my first attempt at a stealth shooter in which I experimented heavily with AI behavior trees, and weapon mechanics. The goal is to sabotage an enemy base full of robot guards without being detected.

Zombies!

Zombies! is my first FPS game built in Unreal Engine 5. It features wave spawners, enemy hitboxes, and player health pickups. The goal is to survive 3 waves of bloodthirsty zombies.

Asylum Escape

Asylum Escape is a first-person surival horror game built in Unity. I experimented heavily with puzzle mechanics and sound design. The goal is to escape the asylum while avoiding 2 strange monsters.
