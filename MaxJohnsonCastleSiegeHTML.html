About Castle Siege

Castle Siege is a third-person medieval-style combat game set in an epic, immersive world. Fight through waves of determined enemies as you lead your army in taking back your castle and claiming victory over your kingdom! Castle Siege features a singleplayer experience blending adventure with fast-paced action, and is praised for its incredible visuals, advanced AI, and cinematic gameplay.

Goal of creating Castle Siege

Castle Siege started off as a small project for me to learn more about Unreal Engine 5's features and practice game development. After creating the terrain, foliage, and castle environment I wanted to test the limits of my skills with a full on combat system along with friendly and enemy AI's that interact with each other and the player's actions. Watching a full scale battle unfold between the NPCs that I made looked so incredible, it was clear I had to turn this into a full sized game.

I came up with a story as I built more of the environment with the main goal being to keep the player engaged and satisfied at all times; I did this through pacing. For example, instead of a long, drawn out introduction Castle Siege provides a short cutscene and throws the player straight into an intense, losing battle followed by a mysterious, underground exploration section to absorb what's happened so far, and to ponder what's next. With extensive playtesting and various feedback from peers I'm incredibly proud of what Castle Siege has become.
